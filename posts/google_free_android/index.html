google free android

31 December, 2019

a few days ago i set my phone up with a lineage without opengapps, and used microg instead. without the playstore and google services, i needed some replacements.

a list.

alternative stores

f-droid - app store with free and open source apps

aurora store - access to free apps in the play store (available in f-droid)

maps

osmand~ - openstreetmap client; works offline!

transportr - public transport app with lots of backends!

openvegemap - find vegetarian and vegan restaurants, based on openstreetmap

weather

forecastie - a nice and simple weather app; uses openweathermap. includes widgets!

mail

k9 mail - a surprisingly usable mail app

calendar

etar - nice calendar app

DAVx⁵ - sync caldav calendars to your phone

messaging

signal - secure messaging. if you want people to switch away from whatsapp, this is probably what comes closest.

conversations - a nice xmpp client

fedilab - a fediverse client; free in f-droid!

security

blokada - filter ads across all your apps!

apps to support free databases

streetcomplete - nice app to fill some of the gaps in openstreetmap. just answer simple questions.

mozilla stumbler - help make mozillas location service better

openfoodfacts - get foodfacts by scanning barcodes on products. also, help fill the database!
